Overview

Purpose

Promoting ocean conservation and protecting South Africa's diverse marine life is our passion. We strive to inspire sustainable practices, raise awareness, and advocate for the preservation of ecosystems vital to coastal communities and the environment's health.

Audience

Dedicated to ocean lovers, conservationists, educators, and eco-conscious individuals who value South Africa's unique marine life. Join a community striving to protect our oceans, inspire change, and ensure a sustainable future for our vibrant underwater ecosystems.

Scenarios

Highlight initiatives for restoring damaged reefs along South Africa's coastline and their impact on marine biodiversity.

Showcase scenarios on tackling plastic waste through community clean-up projects and sustainable solutions.

Discuss efforts to protect endangered species like African penguins, sharks, or whales.

Explore ways eco-friendly tourism can benefit marine life and local communities.

Illustrate how rising temperatures and ocean acidification affect marine ecosystems and what can be done.
